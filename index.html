Confession to Kenji from Japan

You’re golden and unreachable. Everyone wants you and but I like more than your glow.

I like how open you are, how you cling without apology, how sweetness lives in your smallest gestures. I like how you never give up, how your mind is always building forward. You carry qualities I’ve always wished for in myself.

But what pulls me most isn’t only your glow— it’s the shadows you don’t hide. The flaws that match mine, the dark corners we both carry. It makes you real, makes me respect you deeper than any dream could.

I think of you the way I think of stars— from a distance, admired, a little impossible to be with. Yet even with seas between us, I’d still look up, as a soul I already understand— light and darkness both.

Thank you for crossing paths with me. Even if our time was brief, it will remain in my heart like cherry blossoms— beautiful, fleeting, and unforgettable. I hope you find the love you deserve.
